Etch A Scetch of the Future

Identity:

The Etch A Scetch of the future is a product that consists of a board covered in lights that can be fully programmable though an app.

Problem:

The Etch a scetch is outdated for kids these days and who doesnt want to be able to create cool custom light up designs?

Solution:

A board of lights that can be operated through software to create any pattern imaginable.

Target Market:

The target market for my product is kids who want to have fun with an etch a scetch but feel that the old model is too outdated.

Competition:

The only competition for this product is etch a scetch themselves which will clearly be inferior in every way.

Revenue Streams:

The revenue can come from the product itself and then the companion app can have unlockable preset designs for the board that can be purchased for extra money.

Marketing Activities:

In my humble opinion I think this product is so outstanding that no marketing is required for it because as soon as a few people find out about it they'll tell everyone and soon the whole world will know about it.

Expenses:

The expenses to create the product for every sale and the development cost of the companion app.

Team and Key Roles:

My team will easily be able to split up on this project between 2 groups of product designers and software designers to effectively build the best product possible.

Milestones:

We plan to have the hardware part done within 2 months and the softwares barebone features done by march and anything extra we think of adding can be done in the time before its due.
